Northwest Restaurants Inc. is a family owned company that was founded in September of 1994 with the acquisition of thirteen Kentucky Fried Chicken restaurants in Washington. We have grown by acquiring restaurants in the states of Oregon, Idaho, North/South Carolina, and Utah.





Growth has continued through the development of new restaurant locations in these states. The advent of Yum! Brands™ combining different restaurant brands in single locations was embraced by NRI as another means of expanding the company


NRI currently owns and operates 160+ restaurant locations. These include Kentucky Fried Chicken, Taco Bell, Pizza Hut, A&W, and Long John Silver’s brands.





Northwest Restaurants Inc. is headquartered in Woodinville, Washington. We also maintain a regional office in Greenville, North Carolina.
